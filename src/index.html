Ultimate adventure. Rugged everyday style. The world’s most versatile pants are here.

Traditionally, cargo pants were designed by the US Military for paratroopers who literally had to jump out of a plane and be ready for battle as soon as they hit the ground. So we took that inspiration, and made it ready for the everyday man.

SWAT Pants are made for hiking, biking, white-water rafting, horseback riding, snowboarding, rock climbing, or crawling up a mountain on your hands and knees in Ecuador. When you’re done, you can parachute back into everyday life like a paratrooper, and be just as comfortable walking down the street.

Now that’s our idea of “versatile”.

So many pockets, you hardly even need a bag.

When paratroopers land head-first into combat, they need to be able to reach supplies quickly, without even thinking about it. When the British military developed the first iteration of the cargo pant in 1938, it only had one big, square pocket. The Americans improved on that design a few years later with the addition of a few more pockets.

In 2019, we made SWAT Pants with NINE pockets.

There are two zipper side pockets, with two velcro side pockets for added security. There are also three rear pockets for additional supplies, as well as two deep and spacious crossover front pockets. There’s even a climbing carabiner loop - and if you don’t climb - simply use them for your keys.

Waterproof, life proof, and so easy to clean it might as well be dirt proof.

Made with 65% nylon and 35% cotton for the ideal combination of practical style, and form-fitting function. Not only does the Dupont Teflon Treatment repel water, it is also oil resistant and scratch resistant.

We challenge you to rip, tear or scratch these pants. You can’t.

Making the world’s most indestructible pants is about more than one simple layer of anti-tear Dupont Teflon Coating. It’s about understanding how your body moves in extreme situations. It’s about the specialized 3D-tailoring to ensure a free range of movement. It’s about the double-layer of material across the knee. It’s also about the anti-tear triangular section across the crotch.

We tested hundreds of versions of SWAT Pants in the field, with a wide range of activities. This is the version that beat all the others.
